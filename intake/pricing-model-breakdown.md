## CLIENT ACCOUNT STRUCTURE

- Each client gets their OWN ElevenLabs account
- Account created with unique email: clientname@nevermissacall.center
- ElevenLabs manages the limits — when minutes run out, agent stops
- Minute notifications set per client in ElevenLabs dashboard
- No shared plans — no overage risk for GoStar
- Client overages billed through their ElevenLabs account
- You control the agent, they own the minutes
